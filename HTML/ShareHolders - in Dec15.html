December 17, 2015

Dear Investor,

In October, I pitched TEDx Kanata on my 'idea worth spreading'. I told the audience that doing one thing really well can be the key to business success - putting all of your eggs in one basket. As we come to the end of 2015, it's clear that we're doing VoIP service quality really well for Mitel's user base.

Last month, we accepted a Best Ottawa Business (BOB) award, acknowledging Martello's growth and the strength of this strategy. We've been recognized as a key player in Ottawa's technology community, by delivering well-designed SaaS that addresses a growing problem in Mitel's partner community. Our MarWatch software is helping service providers keep customers 'sticky' in competitive hosted voice environments with a differentiator: service quality.

Highlights from Q2-Q3:

Corporate Governance

It was a pleasure to see many of you at our first Annual General Meeting (AGM) in September. You'll find attached to this email more information on the Martello share consolidation and share certificates. We've attracted a high calibre team to our newly elected Board of Directors, led by Chair Sir Terry Matthews, and this news was well received in the local business community. The Board has met twice, and is actively engaged establishing Audit/Compensation committees and awarding stock option grants to employees.

Sales and Revenue Growth

Martello has expanded its global footprint significantly in the first half of Fiscal 2016. Since launching support for Mitel's MiVoice MX-ONE, we've seen traction in Brazil, Germany, Bangladesh and Italy - MarWatch is currently deployed in 20 countries.

Mitel's Premium Software Assurance offering continues to drive recurring revenue for Martello, with more than 600 devices active on MarWatch to date. Traction amongst the service provider community is growing, too: several large service providers are now active MarWatch users. Since adding MarWatch in July 2014, Mitel's attachment rate for the Premium Software Assurance offering to channel partners has increased by 140%.

We continue to work large opportunities, including the Dutch Tax Office and Her Majesty's Revenue & Customs (HMRC). Revenues are strong, with Q2 11% above forecast.

Mitel/Martello Developments

In October we concluded a pricing realignment exercise with Mitel which will see Martello's software embedded in Mitel's Premium Software Assurance subscription and fully Mitel branded in 2016. This provides a consistent delivery model across all Mitel channels, driving sales with greater ordering and pricing simplicity.

Product Development

Released in November, MarWatch 5.1 introduces configurable SNMP device monitoring - telling users not only WHEN there's a problem, but WHERE. This 2 minute video explains why this is important. According to customer Arden Group, "We can now proactively monitor all of the products Arden Group provides its customers from a single platform - MarWatch. Without the proactive performance management solution we have in MarWatch, it would be impossible for us to offer high quality managed services to our customers".

As interest in our solution grows among the MiVoice MX-ONE community, we've added SIP voice quality monitoring to MarWatch, making the software even more valuable.

We're delivering on our commitment to replace Mitel's legacy Enterprise Manager product - launching in early 2016, this will bring an existing base of 2,000+ customers onto our platform.

In 2016, we'll layer analytics capabilities onto MarWatch, commercializing the wealth of customer experience data collected by the system. We'll work towards integrating our software with Mitel endpoints, and continue to expand legacy Aastra device support.

Investment

As we scale rapidly to address our growing opportunity with Mitel, we have kept a close eye on revenues, funding and Martello's cash position. We've submitted the first $300,000 claim under our FedDev funding agreement (Total $750K), and expect an announcement of this project by the Government of Canada early in 2016. As we've grown our development team, Martello's cash position remains strong with $2.7 million in the bank as of November.
